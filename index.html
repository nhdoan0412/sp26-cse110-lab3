New Business

The team discussed building a Study Buddy App that helps students organize assignments, find study partners, and track deadlines.

One idea was to include a calendar page for due dates and a discussion board where students can post study session plans.

Alex suggested that the first version should stay simple and focus on core features only.
Maria agreed and said the team should avoid making the project too large.

Kevin emphasized the importance of clear task ownership so everyone knows what they are responsible for.

Task Assignments

  • Nhan: Draft homepage layout
  • Alex: Research app feature ideas
  • Maria: Create meeting notes template
  • Kevin: Prepare simple project timeline

Miscellaneous Comments / Questions / Concerns

A team member asked whether the app should support mobile screens first or desktop first.

Another concern was whether the team should use plain HTML first before moving to more advanced tools later.

Extra meeting note

The team agreed that for now, focusing on a clean and working basic version is more important than adding extra features.
